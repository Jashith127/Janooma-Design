## 2. Company Profile

**Business model:** Manufactures electrical components (switchgear, LED lighting, MCBs) and sells through a distributor network across India. Currently relies on ~15–20 distributors in key markets, with gaps in South India and Tier-2 cities. Some direct institutional sales to panel builders and contractors.

**Team structure:**
- Ravi (owner) — handles strategy, key distributor relationships, major deals
- Production manager — oversees factory operations
- 3–4 sales staff — handle inquiries, follow-ups, basic order processing
- Accountant — manages Tally, GST filing, payments
- Warehouse/store staff — manage inventory and dispatch

**Sales process:** Primarily inbound. Distributors place orders via phone/WhatsApp. Sales team occasionally does outbound calls to existing contacts. Trade fairs (e.g., Elecrama) generate some new leads. No structured lead generation.

**Procurement process:** Sources raw materials (copper, steel, electronic components) from 5–6 established suppliers. Procurement is relationship-based — Ravi personally negotiates with long-standing suppliers. No formal vendor discovery process.

**Current software stack:**
- Tally Prime (accounting, GST, invoicing)
- WhatsApp Business (primary communication with distributors and suppliers)
- Excel (tracking orders, distributor performance, basic lead lists)
- Email (limited — mostly for formal documentation)

**Communication channels:** WhatsApp (80%+ of business communication), phone calls, in-person meetings. Email used only for formal documentation (POs, contracts).

**Existing distribution model:** Three-tier model — Ravi's company → regional distributors → retailers/panel builders. Distributor network built over 10–15 years through personal relationships, trade fair connections, and word-of-mouth. No systematic process for identifying or onboarding new distributors.

## 12. Product Design Implications

**UX implications:**
- **Mobile-first:** Ravi primarily uses his phone for business. The interface must work flawlessly on mobile.
- **WhatsApp integration:** Leads should appear in WhatsApp without requiring Ravi to log into a dashboard constantly.
- **Simple language:** No technical jargon. "AI worker" and "project" are acceptable metaphors; "Neuro-Symbolic AI" is not.
- **Minimal clicks:** Ravi has low patience for complex workflows. The 6-step process should feel intuitive, not burdensome.

**Onboarding implications:**
- **Zero-setup onboarding:** "Claiming takes two minutes and immediately activates your lead pipeline"
- **Pre-indexed profile:** Ravi should find his company already in the system — instant credibility
- **Guided first project:** Walk Ravi through creating his first project with template examples
- **Human touch:** Founder or team member should guide the first project (alpha program includes this)

**Messaging implications:**
- **Outcome-focused:** "Find 50 distributors" not "AI-powered lead generation platform"
- **Concrete examples:** Use electrical industry examples that mirror Ravi's business
- **Risk reversal:** "No credit card · Free during Alpha · Pay for outcomes"
- **Trust-building:** Highlight "Founder has 30+ years building products" and "direct founder access"
- **Avoid hype:** Ravi is skeptical of tech overpromises. Messaging should feel grounded.

**Pricing implications:**
- **Pay-per-outcome:** Ravi will only pay for results — this is non-negotiable
- **Alpha pricing:** "You choose the price" reduces friction
- **No annual lock-in:** "Cancel anytime"
- **Transparent cost:** Ravi needs to know exactly what a "qualified distributor lead" costs

**Support implications:**
- **WhatsApp support:** Ravi expects to reach someone via WhatsApp, not email
- **Founder access:** Direct founder WhatsApp line is a major trust signal
- **Simple troubleshooting:** Ravi won't read documentation — needs quick, human help

**Potential enterprise requirements:**
- Data privacy assurance: "Your data lives in your private compute slice"
- GST-compliant invoicing for paid outcomes
- Ability to export lead lists for internal tracking
